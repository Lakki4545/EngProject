By Tsapenko Nikita — Paris

French cuisine is known for its long history and meticulous preparation. Many recipes have been passed down through generations and remain relevant today. From aged cheeses to fresh bread and traditional pastries, French cuisine emphasizes quality ingredients and simple cooking techniques..

Well-known French dishes include foie gras, charcuterie, truffles, and handmade chocolate. When preparing these dishes, the focus is on balance and natural flavors. The goal is not complexity, but consistency and respect for tradition.

French cuisine is a specialty that can be served both at home and at formal dinners. It is also often used as a gift. French cuisine is a culture that values good taste, patience, and skill.
